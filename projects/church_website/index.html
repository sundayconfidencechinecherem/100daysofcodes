About Us

Where the Word of God Heals

Grace Abound Ministry is a vibrant, Spirit-filled church dedicated to spreading the healing word of God to all nations.

We believe that God's Word has the power to heal, deliver, and transform lives. Our mandate is to liberate souls through the preaching of the Word of Faith and demonstrate God's love through practical acts of service.
